CONFLICTS
Yacc reports shift-reduce and reduce-reduce con°icts. A shift-reduce con°ict
is a choice between shifting and reducing; a reduce-reduce con°ict is a choice
of reducing by two different rules. By default, Yacc resolves shift-reduce con-
°icts by shifting, and reduce-reduce con°icts by using the rule that appears
earlier in the grammar.
Yacc will report that this
Grammar 3.30
has a shift-reduce con°ict. Any
con°ict is cause for concern, because it may indicate that the parse will not be
as the grammar-designer expected. The con°ict can be examined by reading
